## Description

```
Welkin is a weather app that knows where you actually are.

In Singapore, "it's raining" is useless information — it can pour in Bedok while
Jurong stays dry. Welkin shows the National Environment Agency's two-hour
nowcast for all 47 areas of the island, and when NEA and the global model
disagree about your neighbourhood, Welkin trusts NEA. Tap any area to open its
full forecast without losing your own.

Everywhere else in the world, you get a fast, clean forecast with no ads, no
account, and no tracking.

YOUR CITY, NOT A STOCK PHOTO
Every city gets its own hand-drawn landmark under a live sky — the Merlion, the
Eiffel Tower, Charles Bridge, Machu Picchu, a gopuram, an acacia at sunset. Over
100 cities, and the weather you see behind them is the weather outside.

WHAT YOU GET
• Two-hour nowcast for all 47 Singapore areas, from NEA
• Hourly forecast for the next 24 hours, and 7 days ahead
• Air quality, PM2.5, PM10, ozone, NO2 and UV
• Alerts for rain, haze and thunderstorms, plus a morning briefing
• Home screen and Lock Screen widgets
• Pinpoint location — your neighbourhood, not just "Singapore"
• A fact about wherever you're standing, every day

PRIVACY
Welkin has no account, no ads and no analytics. Your location goes to the
weather service to fetch a forecast, and nowhere else.

Weather and air quality from Open-Meteo. Singapore nowcast from NEA, via
data.gov.sg.
```

## Screenshots

Order matters more than polish — most people never swipe past the second one.

1. **The landmark hero.** Singapore, Merlion or Marina Bay Sands, weather behind
   it. This is the only thing no competitor has. Lead with it.
2. **The Regional Nowcast card**, showing several areas disagreeing with each
   other. This is the "oh, I need that" screenshot for a Singapore audience.
3. **Widgets on a home screen.** "Weather widget" is a high-volume search.
4. Hourly + 7-day forecast.
5. Air quality and alerts.

Add a one-line caption burned into each image. Screenshots are read at thumbnail
size in search results, where the UI itself is illegible.

## Before you submit

- [ ] **App Privacy labels.** Location is collected and *shared with a third
      party* (coordinates go to Open-Meteo). Declare it as "Location — App
      Functionality — not linked to identity, not used for tracking". Getting
      this wrong is a rejection, and a false "no data collected" is worse.
- [ ] **Support URL** is mandatory. If you register a domain, turn on WHOIS
      privacy — otherwise it publishes your name and address.
- [ ] **Copyright field**: set it to the business name, not your own.
- [ ] Age rating: 4+.
- [ ] Confirm the data.gov.sg licence terms still permit this use, and that the
      in-app attribution satisfies them.
- [ ] Enrol as an **Organization** before first release if you don't want your
      legal name on the store page. Converting afterwards means an app transfer.
