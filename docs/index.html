Frequently asked

Why does Welkin ask for my location?
Only to show the forecast where you are. You can deny it and search for a city instead. Your location is never stored or shared — see our Privacy Policy.

Where does the weather data come from?
From Open-Meteo, an open weather data source.

The forecast looks wrong or won’t load.
Check your internet connection and that location access is enabled in iOS Settings → Welkin. Pull down on the main screen to refresh.
